## Project Overview
**AFRICAN ROOTS** is a multi-page website designed to connect and empower people to rediscover their heritage and learn about other tribes’ traditional activities and culture. The platform promotes cultural awareness, unity, and appreciation by encouraging users to explore traditions and submit enquiries/contact the team.

## Website Goals and Objectives
### Goals
- Celebrate African heritage, culture, and identity.
- Help users understand and appreciate different tribes’ traditions.
- Provide a simple, accessible website experience for users to navigate and learn.

### Objectives
- Provide clear information on the website’s purpose (Home/About).
- Present the service offerings to users (Services).
- Allow users to contact the organization and submit enquiries (Contact/Enquiry form).
- Ensure the site is responsive and visually consistent using a shared stylesheet.

## Key Features and Functionality
- **Navigation/Header Menu** across pages (Home, About, Services, Contact, Enquiry).
- **Home page** content welcoming users to AFRICAN ROOTS and explaining the concept.
- **About page** describing the mission and vision of the project.
- **Services page** outlining what is offered to users.
- **Contact page** with contact details and a location link.
- **Enquiry Form page** for users to submit details.
  - Uses `enquiry-handler.js` to simulate submission feedback (no backend required).
  - Shows status messages and resets the form after submission.
- **Shared Styling** via `style.css` for consistent layout, typography, and backgrounds.
- **Responsive Design** using CSS media queries to support tablets and mobile screens.
